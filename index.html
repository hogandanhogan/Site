I am currently working on a social media app called ProxiMap I have been practicing Software Development for over a year. Apart from Objective-C and Swift, I am also familiar with HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and Python

I am pivoting from a successful career as a Chemist in the Spirits Industry. What was missing in all this fun was a personal connection to the analysis I was performing. Enter Software Development. Once I began learning to code, I quickly realized that I could take my programming experience and apply it to build something visual and personally tangible.

I am always fascinated by technology and am constantly learning new techniques. I love the logic of software development and the challenge of finding the best tools and algorithms to build new and amazing things.

RECENT | PROJECTS

ProxiMap

A social media app that allows users to view other users post in a map view. The user can filter by post subject and message users and send push notifications to other users.

Pixure

An alternative layout for iPhone photo library. The app allows users to view their camera roll photos in an aesthetically pleasing layout with less margin than the existing iOS photo library.

AlarMock

An alarm clock that wakes users with mockery. The app loads and caches jokes dynamically with Parse cloud service. The level of mockery increases as the user continues to snooze.
